Our Story

A Legacy of Trust and Innovation

ANBI Tech Solution was established with a clear vision: to bring world-class security technology to Nepal. What began as a small team of passionate engineers in Kathmandu has evolved into one of the country's most respected security and ELV solution providers.

Over the past 15 years, we have successfully completed more than 500 projects across residential, commercial, industrial, and government sectors. Our portfolio spans from single-family home installations to large-scale enterprise security deployments.

We believe that security is not just about installing cameras and alarms — it is about creating peace of mind. Every solution we design is built around this principle, combining cutting-edge technology with human-centered service.
